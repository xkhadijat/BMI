If you are not satisfied with your BMI results and would like to improve your BMI, here are some tips ranging from Beginner - Expert Level

Beginner Tips

  • Aim for a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.
  • Incorporate physical activity into your daily routine, such as walking, biking, or dancing.
  • Limit sugary drinks, up the intake of water.

Intermediate Tips

  • Track your food intake and physical activity to become more aware of your habits.
  • Experiment with different types of exercise, such as strength training, yoga, or high-intensity interval training (HIIT).
  • Practice mindful eating by paying attention to hunger and fullness cues.

Expert Tips

  • Consider working with a personal trainer to design a customized workout plan.
  • Focus on building muscle through resistance training to boost metabolism and improve body composition.
  • Stay consistent with your healthy habits and be patient with your progress.
